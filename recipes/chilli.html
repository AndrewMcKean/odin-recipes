Vegetarian Chilli

Description

Are you a newly converted vegetarian climbing the walls for that meaty texture you're missing? Well climb no more! Simply preparing the easy recipe down below will have your meaty cravings sated with 100% less animal murder!

Ingredients

  • 1 500g pack of veggie mince
  • 1/2 a can of kidney beans
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato puree
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chilli powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. In a large pot, combine veggie mince all of the above ingredients.
  2. Heat at gas mark 6 until bubbling.
  3. Once the mixture begins to bubble turn down the heat and let simmer for at least an hour"
