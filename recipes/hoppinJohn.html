Hoppin' John

Description

Are you also not American and have absolutely no idea what Hoppin' John is?! Read on to find out! It had 4/5 stars on allrecipes.com and was apparently accepted to Harvard College Dining Services so it's probably good!

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup long grain rice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cans black-eyed peas, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 teaspoon cajun seasoning

Method

  1. Bring broth and rice to boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the rice is tender and liquid has been absorbed. This should take around 20 -25 minutes.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium-high heat. Saute onion in hot oil until translucent, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Stir in the rice, black-eyed peas, and Cajun seasoning.
  4. Cover the pot with a lid and cook until flavours blend, about 10 minutes.
  5. Et voila! Dinner is served.
